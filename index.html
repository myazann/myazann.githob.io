About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Hogeschool van Amsterdam and Leiden University, focusing on designing chatbots that adapt their conversation styles based on context to enhance user experience. My research involves personalization of Large Language Models (LLMs), domain adaptation in low-resource scenarios, and consumer psychology related to chatbot interactions.

Research Areas

  • Personalization of LLMs and chatbots
  • Domain adaptation in low-resource scenarios
  • Consumer psychology in chatbot interactions
